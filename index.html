Our Story

How We Met

In the beautiful tradition of families coming together, Sijin and Prathyusha were introduced by their elders. What began as a simple meeting soon felt warm and natural, as conversations flowed easily and smiles were shared. With the quiet joy of both families, it felt as though two paths that were meant to meet had finally come together.

The Promise

With the blessings of parents, elders, and loved ones, their nischayam was joyfully fixed. Surrounded by family who had watched them grow, Sijin and Prathyusha happily said yes to a lifetime together. What began through tradition soon blossomed into a bond of understanding, warmth, and happiness — a new journey they now begin with grateful hearts. 💛
